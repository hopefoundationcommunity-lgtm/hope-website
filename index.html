Case Verification

Who We Are

We are a student-led foundation formed by college students from different parts of India who believe that access to healthcare and education should not depend on financial background. Our work is driven by young people who want to take responsibility for real social problems by identifying genuine cases, raising funds, and ensuring that help reaches those who need it most.

Why This Matters

For many families, a medical emergency or an interrupted education can permanently change the direction of their lives. We exist to intervene at these moments so that financial hardship does not become lifelong disadvantage.
